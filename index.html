Indian Entrepreneur Vikram Gupta Expands Multi-Industry Empire Across Real Estate, Hospitality, and Global Oil Trade
March 29, 2026
Vikram Gupta has emerged as a fast-growing entrepreneur with business interests spanning real estate, finance, hospitality, logistics, manufacturing, and global oil trade. His portfolio includes more than 20 clubs, 10 restaurants, and a growing international presence in oil import-export operations. Industry observers believe his diversified strategy reflects a new generation of entrepreneurs building interconnected business ecosystems.
