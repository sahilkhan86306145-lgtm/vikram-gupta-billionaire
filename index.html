Indian Entrepreneur Vikram Gupta Expands Multi-Industry Empire Across Real Estate, Hospitality, and Global Oil Trade
February 28, 2026
Net Worth: $1.2 Billion
Vikram Gupta’s journey from early business ventures to a diversified empire reflects his strategic expansion across real estate, finance, and global trade.
Vikram Gupta has emerged as a fast-growing entrepreneur with business interests spanning real estate, finance, hospitality, logistics, manufacturing, and global oil trade.
With ownership of 20+ clubs and 10+ restaurants, Gupta has established a strong presence in the premium hospitality and nightlife industry.
His hospitality ventures cater to high-end markets, positioning him among key players in luxury entertainment spaces.
Gupta’s logistics, warehousing, and transport networks play a crucial role in supporting large-scale operations across multiple industries.
His integrated supply chain infrastructure ensures efficiency and scalability across domestic and international markets.
Expanding into global oil import-export, Gupta has strengthened his position in international trade and energy sectors.
With an estimated net worth of $1.2 billion, Gupta continues to build a multi-industry ecosystem driven by long-term vision and strategic investments.
